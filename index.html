Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I join a league? — Tap Leagues → Join League and enter the invite code shared by your league admin.
  • How does fantasy scoring work? — Goals (5–7 pts by position), assists (3 pts), clean sheets (5 pts), tackles, key passes, and shots on target all count. Your captain's total is doubled.
  • How do I raise the pot? — In the Leagues → Bet tab, open the Raise window during an active tournament phase. All members must accept for the raise to go through.
  • Can I change my squad? — Yes, transfers are available between matches via the Team tab.
  • Is real money involved? — No. The betting pool is fictional paper money for friendly competition only.
  • How do I delete my account? — Go to Profile → Delete Account. All your data is permanently erased.
