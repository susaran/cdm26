# Privacy Policy

**Gallardigan$ WC 2026 Fantasy Pool**  
Last updated: May 2026

---

## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Gallardigan$ WC 2026 Fantasy Pool ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By creating an account and using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

- **Account information:** display name, email address, and optional profile photo.
- **League activity:** fantasy squad selections, match score predictions, trade proposals, and transfer history.
- **Communications:** messages sent within leagues and bulletin board posts.
- **Device information:** device type and operating system version, collected automatically by Firebase Analytics to improve app performance.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate and improve the App:

- Authenticate your account and display your profile to league members.
- Calculate fantasy and prediction scores.
- Enable social features such as trading, bulletin boards, and direct messages.
- Send push notifications for match events, trades, and league activity (only with your permission).
- Analyse anonymous, aggregated usage patterns to improve the App.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third parties.

## 4. Data Sharing

Your display name, profile photo, and in-league statistics are visible to other members of leagues you have joined. No personal data is shared outside the App except as required by law or as described below.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

- **Google Firebase** (Authentication, Firestore Database, Cloud Storage, Cloud Messaging, Analytics) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- **API-Football** — to retrieve World Cup 2026 match fixtures and player statistics.
- **Google Sign-In / Sign in with Apple** — if you choose social login, your name and email are provided to us by those services.

## 6. Paper Money & No Real-Money Gambling

The betting pool feature in this App uses fictional paper money only. No real currency is collected, transferred, or paid out through the App at any time. This feature is provided purely for entertainment and friendly competition among league members.

## 7. Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications for match kick-offs, trade activity, new messages, and league updates. You may withdraw permission at any time via your device's notification settings (Settings → Notifications → Gallardigan$) or within the App under Profile → Match Reminders.

## 8. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account (Profile → Delete Account), all personally identifiable data — including your squad, predictions, messages, and achievements — is permanently deleted. This action cannot be undone.

## 9. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption). Firebase enforces server-side security rules that restrict access to your data to you and members of leagues you have joined. We do not store passwords; authentication is handled by Firebase or your chosen social login provider.

## 10. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

## 11. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Correct inaccurate information via your profile settings.
- Delete your account and all associated data at any time.
- Withdraw consent for push notifications at any time.

To exercise these rights, use the tools in the App (Profile screen) or contact us at the address below.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
